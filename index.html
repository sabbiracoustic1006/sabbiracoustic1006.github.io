About

Hi, I'm Sabbir Ahmed, a third-year CS PhD student at SUNY Binghamton. My research focuses on the Security and Efficiency of Deep Learning, with extensive experience in both Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing (NLP).

Before starting my PhD, I completed my MS in EE from the University of California Riverside and my BSc in EEE from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology. I also worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at REVE Systems, Bangladesh, where I contributed to various AI-driven projects involving both Computer Vision and NLP.

My research has been published in multiple top-tier AI/ML conferences including CVPR and ICCV.
